Welcome to Great Composers, your one stop shop for all things related to your favorite classical composers.

We love these five composers for their mastery of the following criteria:

  1. Originality - The composer took music in new and exciting directions
  2. Impact - How greatly the composer influenced the musical scene, both in their lifetime and in years/centuries to come
  3. Craftmanship - From a technical point of view, how brilliantly the composer's music was constructed
  4. Sheer Enjoyability - Quite simply, how much pleasure the composer's music brings
